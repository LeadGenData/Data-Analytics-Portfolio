# Automation Scripts & outreach Campaigns

Python and PowerShell utilities designed to automate data collection, lead normalization, and CRM syncing.

## ⚙️ Process Flow
1. **`scrape_restaurants.py`** — Scrapes local businesses to harvest contact details and operational metrics.
2. **`clean_leads.py`** — Validates phone numbers, normalizes lead casing, and deduplicates records.
3. **`sync_sent_from_outlook.ps1`** — Connects to local Outlook accounts, parses sent folders, captures bounces, and synchronizes statuses back to the CRM database.
