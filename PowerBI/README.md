# Business Intelligence & Reporting (Power BI)

Detailed metrics and analytical reporting dashboards designed to provide executive decision-makers with core operational insights.

## 📊 Highlighted Dashboards
1. **Hospital Emergency Room Intelligence** — Analyzes clinical ER admissions, average patient wait times, triage priority levels, and customer satisfaction scores. Identifies bottlenecks in patient processing.
2. **Chocolate Sales Analytics** — Evaluates regional sales volumes, supply chain shipment statistics, and product profitability margins.
